Understanding Your Registration Fees

California vehicle registration fees are based on your vehicle's value, type, county, and renewal status. Here's what each fee covers:

🚗 Registration Fee

The base fee to register your vehicle with the CA DMV. Amount varies by vehicle type and weight class.

🏷️ Vehicle License Fee (VLF)

Charged at 0.65% of your vehicle's market value. You may be eligible for a state income tax deduction on this fee.

🏛️ County / District Fees

Local fees set by your county for special programs, transportation improvements, and local services.

💨 Smog Abatement Fee

A state fee that funds clean air programs. Most vehicles older than 4 years require a smog inspection every 2 years.

⚠️ Delinquency Fees

Late renewal fees apply after your expiration date. Renewing on time avoids penalties of 60%–80% of your base fee.

🔧 Service Fee

Our processing fee for this online convenience service. All DMV fees are passed through at cost with no markup.

Important Information

📋
Planned Non-Operation (PNO): The DMV will accept a PNO filing up to 60 days before your registration expires, or up to 90 days after it expires. If your vehicle will not be operated on public roads, filing a PNO avoids full registration fees.

⏰ Grace Period

California provides a renewal window before and after your expiration date, but late fees apply once expired. Renew before your due date to save money.

📧 Renewal Reminders

Enter your email when renewing and we'll automatically remind you before next year's expiration — so you'll never miss a deadline.

💨 Smog Inspections

Vehicles older than 4 years in most California counties require a biennial smog check. Your vehicle must pass before registration can be renewed.

🔖 Lost or Stolen Stickers

If your registration sticker or certificate is lost or stolen, file CA DMV Form REG 56 (Application for Replacement Plates, Stickers, Documents) or contact us.

Frequently Asked Questions

What do I need to renew my registration online?
You need your license plate number, the last 5 digits of your VIN (found on your dashboard, door jamb, or registration card), your county, and a valid credit card.
When will I receive my registration sticker?
You'll receive a digital confirmation immediately after payment. Your physical sticker and card will be mailed to your DMV address on file within 7–10 business days.
Can I renew if my registration is already expired?
Yes. You can renew after expiration, but late fees will be added. We recommend renewing as soon as possible to minimize penalties.
Do I need a smog check before renewing?
If required for your vehicle, a smog check must be completed before renewal. Your DMV renewal notice will indicate if one is needed.
Is my payment and personal information secure?
Yes. Payments are processed by Stripe (PCI-DSS Level 1 certified). We never store your card details. All data is protected with 256-bit SSL encryption.
